What We Offer

Professional Detailing Services

Every service is performed by trained detailing specialists using professional-grade products — brought directly to your location.

Exterior Hand Wash & Wax

A thorough two-bucket hand wash, clay bar treatment, and hand-applied carnauba wax to protect and restore your vehicle's shine.

  • Two-bucket hand wash method
  • Clay bar decontamination
  • Hand wax & tire shine
  • Window cleaning

Interior Deep Clean

Complete interior restoration — vacuuming, steam cleaning, leather conditioning, and odor elimination for a fresh-from-the-factory feel.

  • Full vacuum & compressed air blow-out
  • Steam cleaning seats & carpets
  • Leather conditioning
  • Dashboard & console detailing

Full Detail Package

The complete inside-and-out transformation. Combines our exterior wash & wax with a full interior deep clean for a total vehicle refresh.

  • Complete exterior wash & wax
  • Full interior deep clean
  • Engine bay rinse
  • Tire & rim detailing

Ceramic Coating

Long-lasting nano-ceramic protection that bonds to your paint, creating a hydrophobic shield against UV, scratches, and environmental contaminants.

  • 2–5 year protection lifespan
  • Hydrophobic water repellency
  • UV & oxidation resistance
  • High-gloss finish

Paint Correction

Professional machine polishing to remove swirl marks, light scratches, water spots, and oxidation — restoring your paint to a mirror-like finish.

  • Single & multi-stage polishing
  • Swirl & scratch removal
  • Water spot elimination
  • Paint depth measurement

Engine Bay Detailing

Safe, thorough engine compartment cleaning using eco-friendly degreasers to remove grime buildup — leaving your engine looking factory fresh.

  • Eco-friendly degreaser treatment
  • Safe pressure rinse
  • Plastic & rubber conditioning
  • Hose & cable detailing
